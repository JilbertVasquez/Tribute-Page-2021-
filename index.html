Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German scientist and mathematician who pioneered the special and general theories of relativity. For his explanation of the photoelectric phenomenon, he received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921. After being targeted by the German Nazi Party, he emigrated to the United States in the next decade.
